How It Works

From Notes to Narrative Panels

01

Paste or Upload

Type notes directly or add a `.txt` / `.pdf` file up to 5 MB.

02

Extract Ideas

Cominote cleans the content, finds concepts, and builds simple semantic links.

03

Build Scenes

A guided three-act flow turns the ranked ideas into comic-friendly beats.

04

Render and Review

The final strip appears inline with concepts, scenes, and download actions.

Supported Subjects

Built for Multi-Subject Learning

Science

Labs, cells, forces, ecosystems, energy flow, and concept explanations.

History

Timelines, events, leaders, causes, and consequences in narrative order.

Mathematics

Definitions, formulas, worked ideas, and revision-friendly explanations.

Literature

Themes, settings, characters, and plot flow transformed into visual story beats.

Inside the Product

What the Finished Mini Project Includes

M-01 Input Module

Title, subject, visual style, text area, file upload, and descriptive validation.

M-02 to M-04 Story Engine

Lightweight preprocessing, concept ranking, relation hints, and narrative sequencing.

M-05 Comic Renderer

Programmatic panel generation with comic colours, avatars, speech bubbles, and captions.

M-06 Output Module

Preview image, download actions, recent history, and explainable scene summaries.
